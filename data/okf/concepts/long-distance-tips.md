# Definition

Long-distance relationship tips are strategies for maintaining emotional connection, trust, and relationship health when partners live in different cities or countries.

# Common goals

- Maintain emotional intimacy despite physical distance
- Build shared experiences and rituals remotely
- Plan realistically for eventually closing the distance gap
