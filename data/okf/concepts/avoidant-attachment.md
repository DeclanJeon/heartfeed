# Definition

Avoidant attachment is a relational style characterized by discomfort with closeness, valuing independence highly, and tendency to suppress emotional needs. People with avoidant attachment often pull away when a relationship deepens.

# Common goals

- Identifying avoidant patterns early in dating
- Understanding when avoidant behavior signals incompatibility vs. fear
- Learning strategies for communicating needs to avoidant partners
