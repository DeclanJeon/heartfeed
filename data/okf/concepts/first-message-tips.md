# Definition

First message tips are strategies for crafting opening messages on dating apps that increase the likelihood of a response and positive engagement.

# Common goals

- Stand out from generic "hey" or "hi" messages
- Demonstrate genuine interest and profile engagement
- Start a conversation that flows naturally
