# Definition

Breakup recovery is the process of emotional healing, self-rediscovery, and rebuilding after the end of a romantic relationship.

# Common goals

- Process grief in a healthy timeframe
- Rebuild self-identity outside the relationship
- Learn from the experience without dwelling
- Prepare emotionally for future healthy relationships
