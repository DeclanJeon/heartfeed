# Definition

Red flags are warning signs or behaviors that indicate potential problems in a dating partner. Recognizing red flags early helps avoid unhealthy or abusive relationships.

# Common goals

- Identify manipulative or controlling behaviors early
- Distinguish between minor incompatibilities and genuine danger signs
- Build confidence in trusting instincts about uncomfortable situations
