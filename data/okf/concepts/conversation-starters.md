# Definition

Effective conversation starters are questions and topics that facilitate natural, engaging dialogue during dates and early dating stages.

# Common goals

- Reduce awkwardness and create comfortable atmosphere
- Discover genuine compatibility through meaningful conversation
- Balance personal sharing with active listening
