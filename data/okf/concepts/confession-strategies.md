# Definition

Confession strategies are approaches for expressing romantic feelings to someone, balancing honesty with emotional risk management.

# Common goals

- Express feelings clearly without overwhelming the other person
- Manage personal expectations and emotional risk
- Preserve the existing relationship regardless of outcome
